Our invention originated from the wish to track the hidden carts inside a building, since many people don't feel obligated to return them after usage. We aim to design and implement an indoor object tracking device -- Cart-E.

Cart-E is specifically designed to track indoor positions. Cart-E uses open-source information such as WIFI-router's public IP addresses to increase accuracy.

With our in-door tracking device Cart-E, which is small, inexpensive, and energy-efficient, we can locate desired objects inside a building simply through accessing this website. This technology produces an affordable device with little maintenance requirement that provides detailed information about the object location in 3D space.
